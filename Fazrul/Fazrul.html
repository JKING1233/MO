Fazrul - Trainer Profile

Fazrul

Fitness Manager | Senior Coach

About Me: With nearly 10 years in the fitness industry, I’ve had the privilege of helping hundreds of clients smash their goals — from getting stage-ready physiques to completely transforming their lifestyles. My passion for bodybuilding fuels my own competitive journey, but I believe there’s more to fitness than just looking good.

I call myself a functional bodybuilder because it’s not enough to look strong — you should be able to move well too. There’s no point in having an amazing physique if you can’t use it to live freely and pain-free.

I love guiding people to realize their true potential, helping them understand what their bodies are capable of, and breaking through the barriers holding them back. Training, to me, is both a science and an art — understanding biomechanics, movement patterns, and the root causes of pain or dysfunction allows me to create long-term solutions, not just quick fixes.

Outside the gym, I’m a curious observer of people and movement — whether that’s during my long walks, cycling sessions, or a quiet coffee moment spent people-watching. And yes, I’m oddly competitive with the NY Times Wordle — keeps my brain as sharp as my lifts.
